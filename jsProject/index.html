Participant: Valérie Hellmüller

More information

General Information: The project is an Interdisciplinary Project of Geomatics MS.c. Program at ETH Zurich, conducted by Daniel Laumer and Haojun Cai. It is supported by the Institute of Cartography and Geoinformation and importantly, all active participants, and Google Map Timeline function.
About Project: To take advantage of everyday Google Timeline data, we provide summary information on spatial and temporal aspects, including basic statistics, the 24-hour timeline of the top-visited places, aggregated trajectories and schematic view, home work balance, and commuting habits. To be continued in the future.
